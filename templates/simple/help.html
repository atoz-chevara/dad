How to help Debian

If you want to help Debian to be better everyday, here are some tips to help out.

  • Promote the use of Debian.
  • Report and fix bugs .
  • Join the DDTP and translate all Debian Package Descriptions.
  • Get involved in one of the Debian Teams.
  • Find out other development tasks.
  • If you want to help by giving money to Debian, please visit the donations page.
  • Encourage developers to keep doing a good job by sending a thank you for them :)
